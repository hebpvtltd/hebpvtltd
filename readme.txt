HOW TO HOST THIS WEBSITE:
1. Visit https://www.netlify.com and sign up or log in.
2. Unzip this folder.
3. Drag and drop the files into your Netlify dashboard.
4. Connect your domain (heb.com.np).
5. In Cloudflare DNS, add:
   - A record for @ pointing to 75.2.60.5
   - CNAME for www pointing to your-site-name.netlify.app
